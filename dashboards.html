Programmed a multi-page Dashboard featuring comparison of Machine Learning models and predicting best playing position of any Soccer player based on data provided by EA Sports in their 2021 releases. Added an optimization algorithm to choose best team based on constraints as specified by user.

This dynamic dashboard offers a variety of data visualization options to analyze the electric vehicle market in India. It provides insights into market size, key players, and nationwide adoption trends. It also highlights the performance of various time series models in forecasting electric vehicle sales for different manufacturers, offering a comprehensive view of market dynamics and future potential.

This is a Shiny-based interactive multi-page user dashboard that enables users to customize parameters and compare the performance of various time series models, facilitating accurate predictions of the time required to achieve any percentage of renewable energy penetration in the United States.

This Shiny Visualization dashboard utilizes machine learning and time series models to predict the sales and profit of an E-commerce SuperStore. The users can use the dashboard to fit their own data and check expected sales and profit growth. The machine learning models can also predict the Category of commodity being sold with almost 80% accuracy.
